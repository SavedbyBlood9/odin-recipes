Taco

Description

The perfect ground beef taco recipe! Taking just a little extar time to make homemade taco seasoning makes all the difference; spoon into flour or corn tortillas and serve with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons dried minced onion
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (Opitonal)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (Optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried minced garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Mix minced onion, salt, chili powder, cornstarch, cumin, red pepper flakes, cayenne pepper, minced garlic, and oregano in a bowl; set seasoning mix aside.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef in the got skillet until browned and crumbly, 7 to 10 minutes. Drain and discard any excess grease.
  4. Pour water and seasoning mix over beef; stir to combine. Bring to a simmer and cook until mixture thickens slightly, about 5 minutes.
